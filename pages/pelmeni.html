Pelmeni Recipe

Like mini dumplings, but better.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 1 cup of pepper
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of oil

Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
  3. Stir
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
  5. Stir
  6. Enjoy!
