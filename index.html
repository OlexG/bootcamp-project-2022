Welcome to Oleks's Kitchen, where we cook traditional Ukrainian Food

Varenyky

These half moons of dough may be filled with a variety of fruits, vegetables, meats and cheeses, although for Christmas Eve potato and cabbage fillings are the favorite.

Borscht

everyone knows what it is and many people around the world have fallen in love with this iconic beet soup.

Pelmeni

Pelmeni are little plump meat dumplings made from a hand mold (called the pelmennitsa.) It makes a hearty batch that you can freeze for later! An Eastern European Comfort food, dolloped with sour cream!
